Friday Thoughts


Its never clear how I feel about Fridays. Because on the one hand at least it's the weekend, but on the other hand its almost always the day with the most amount of requirements due. I personally think the idea of rest is enough to get me through all the work we need to do but even more than that the friends I have that go through all this with me.

Some of the subjects on this day are pretty tough. I really struggle in Math and Computer science because I didn't have any of the grade 7 & 8 lessons, but do I try, and even if I fail over and over I will try again. The teachers and classmates I have everyday also make this day more bearable so its still okay. If there isn't anything to do though just listening to the teachers will always be a good day because I still learn something new and they make it easier with how kind and open they are to those who ask questions.

Overall, Fridays are the intro to the weekend and when done right play with your favorite music. Just dont overstress, relax and have fun so you can be ready for next week!
